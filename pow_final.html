Is there a connection between highest parental education and students' SAT

The 2021 College Board Standardized Aptitude Test (SAT) statistics are out and they include the highest level of parental education. How much parental education influences their child’s measured intelligence is constantly being discussed. Especially when parental education may correlate with income levels, which means affect to standardized testing study resources. This data project’s finds out whether or not there is a link between the highest level of parental education and the individual’s mean SAT score.

Project by Maya Pow
